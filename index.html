Vintage Casio Watches

This website is about the vintage casio watches and their uniqe design

Casio Vintage watches are iconic for their retro, minimalist design. With rectangular or square cases, slim profiles, and digital displays framed by gold, silver, or black stainless steel, they blend nostalgia with elegance. The bracelet-style straps, often in mesh or link designs, add to their timeless appeal, making them versatile accessories for casual and stylish outfits alike.


Some popular casio vintage models:

  • LA680WA-1
  • A159W-N1
  • ABL100WEG-9AVT
  • AQ230A-7AVT



The Casio LA680WA-1 is a compact, retro digital watch with a square stainless steel case and bracelet.
It features a black digital display with essential functions like time, date, alarm, and stopwatch. Its sleek,
lightweight design offers both comfort and vintage style.

The A159W-N1 is a stylish and compact digital watch that combines retro aesthetics with modern functionality. Featuring a stainless steel case and a classic resin band, it boasts a vibrant digital display that shows the time, date, and has an alarm feature. With its lightweight design, the A159W-N1 offers comfortable wear for everyday use, making it a perfect accessory for those who appreciate vintage charm and practicality.
